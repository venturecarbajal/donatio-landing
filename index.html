PHILANTHROPY x TECHNOLOGY

We are firm believers of philanthropy and technology. On our journey looking for a far better way to give to the causes we love, and to connect beyond letters and emails that we couldn't connect well through, we gained the inspiration to build Donatio. The idea of using Donatio is not to donate hundreds or thousands of dollars all at once. Rather, our goal is to leverage peopleâ€™s shopping habits for good by empowering consumers to make small donations by rounding up their change or donating a small percentage of their total cart to charity. Over time, these small and sporadic donations will add up and help transform a new way of giving. Philanthropists and nonprofits both deserve better so we are building something that we would love using, and we hope you do too!
